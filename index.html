My Journey

Hii I am Narayan Vyas from Washim Maharashtra I am a cyclist I ride up to very long distances. I started cycling for physical fitness but later I made it my passion... In the year 2017 I did some events based on time limits. After the events I had decided that I could do more long distance cycling. I did 1800km cycle ride from central India to Wagha border. After that successful ride for my motherland I did 2000km cycle ride from central India to Ramsetu ... These all journeys and events are very exciting but after these I thought that I should ride for India's pride as whenever we talk about our India, it's culture, it's achievement and also the achievers how can we forget the name of the God of cricket Mr. Sachin Tendulkar sir Sachin sir is the most inspiring personality ever. When he played for India, all the Indians had a special reason to watch cricket and that reason was Sachin sir it's like when Sachin sir plays we shout his name "Sachin at our homes in front of television... Watching him on the field is our favourite craziness... So, I decided to do a cycle ride for him as I am very much inspired by his hard work and his whole journey from Sachin to Bharat Ratna Master Blaster Sachin... I decided my route from India gate to gateway of India and named the event as "Ride For the Pride of India 45 days before this event I started my diet routine I prepared myself mentally for that ride. It was tough but it became easy just because I thought about Sachin sir's whole hard work and journey and the day when I met him I saw him it was the most memorable and awaited moment for me.

The best part of the whole meet up is when he asked me about my safety my arrangements the way he cared for me as a sports person. The moment when he gave me autograph I still can't believe I hadan autograph from Sachin Sir, I had the most memorable moments with the Pride of India I never forget that Ride, the moment when I stood with sir, that experience was an unforgettable experience ever.
